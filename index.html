Vishal Purohit

ECE Ph.D. Student, Purdue University

Hi! I am a 3rd year Ph.D. student in the ECE program at Purdue University, where I work with Prof. Qiang Qiu. My research aims to develop novel methods and algorithms for learning high-dimensional signals from sparse and quantized measurements, with applications in computer vision.

Before joining Purdue, I was a research intern at IIT Varanasi for a year, where I worked on meta-learning and federated learning projects. Previously, I worked as an engineer in the medical electronics division at Tata Elxsi for 2 years, where I developed software and hardware solutions for medical devices.

Research Interests

  • Generative Models (2D and 3D) - GANs, Score-Based Models, VAEs
  • (Multimodal) Large Language Models
  • Inverse Problems
